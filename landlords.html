Landlords

There is a legal duty on owners of residential rented properties to have annual safety checks carried out on the gas appliances they provide for their tenants' use. We will carry out annual safety checks on your rental properties issuing the necessary gas safety certificate (CP12) to you and your tenant. We will also send a reminder to you when your next annual service is due, giving you peace of mind that your gas safety legal obligations are covered.
